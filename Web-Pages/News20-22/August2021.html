1 August[edit]

  • Canada has reported 897 new cases, bringing the total number to 1,432,338. Canada also reported 2 new deaths bringing the death toll to 26,600.[1]

There are 36 active cases.

  • Ukraine has reported 484 new daily cases and six new daily deaths, bringing the total number to 2,253,269 and 52,951 respectively; a total of 2,186,994 patients have recovered.[8]

  • Australian former Cricketer and London Spirit head coach Shane Warne has tested positive for COVID-19 and was prompted to go into self-isolation.[10]
