Pandemic chronology[edit]

1 March[edit]

  • The Canadian province of Ontario reported 1,023 new cases.[1][2]
  • Malaysia has reported 1,828 new cases, bringing the total number to 302,580. There are 2,486 recoveries, bringing the total number of recoveries to 275,903. There are five new deaths, bringing the death toll to 1,135. There are 25,542 active cases, with 198 in intensive care and 90 on ventilator support.[3]
  • New Zealand has reported two new cases, bringing the total number to 2,378 (2,022 confirmed and 356 probable). The number of recoveries remains 2,285 and the death toll remains 26. There are 67 active cases (55 at the border and 12 community transmissions).[4]
  • Singapore has reported 12 new imported cases, bringing the total to 59,948.[5] Seven have been discharged, bringing the total number of recoveries to 59,823. The death toll remains at 29.
